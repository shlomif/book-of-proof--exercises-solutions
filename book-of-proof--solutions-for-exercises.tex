\subsection{Exercise 1}

Given: x is even.

x is even so $x = 2a$ for some $ a \in Z $ . Therefore,
$ x^2 = (2a)^2 = 4a^2 = 2\cdot(2\cdot a^2)$. As a result, $x^2 = 2b$
for some integer $b = 2 \cdot a^2 $ and is even.
